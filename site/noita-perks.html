Noita Perk List

Tier 1 (★)
Tier 2 (★★)
Tier 3 (★★★)
Tier 4 (★★★★)
Tier 5 (★★★★★)

Tier 1:

  • Worm Detractor
    • Worms of the world run from you

  • Worm Attractor
    • Worms of the world come to you

  • Toxic Sludge Immunity
    • You take no damage from toxic sludge.

  • Strong Levitation
    • You can fly 100% longer.

  • Slime Blood
    • You bleed slime

  • Repelling Cape
    • Stains drop at a fast rate (when moving).

  • Oil Blood
    • You bleed fast burning oil

  • More Love
    • Creatures in the world are more friendly towards each other.

  • Low Gravity
    • Your movement is floatier and you jump and fly higher.

  • Glass Cannon
    • Your spells deal 5x damage and have larger blast ranges, but your maximum health is capped to 50.

  • Freeze Field
    • Freezes liquid around you

  • Fast Swimming
    • You are an expert swimmer

  • Explosion immunity
    • You take no direct damage from explosions.

  • Edit Wands Everywhere
    • Divine blessing allows you to edit wands everywhere.

  • Boomerang Spells
    • Almost all your spells arc towards you; you have higher projectile resistance

Tier 2:

  • Vampirism
    • You regain health from drinking blood, but you lose one third of maximum health.

  • Trick Greed
    • 4x instead of 2x gold is dropped when death is an accident.

  • Teleportitis
    • Damage is reduced by 20%. Every time you take damage, you teleport away.

  • Stainless Armour
    • You take 50% less damage as long as you have no active stain status effect.

  • Revenge Explosion
    • Often when you take damage, you release a magical explosion that hurts enemies.

  • More Hatred
    • Creatures in the world are more aggressive toward each other.

  • Meelee Immunity
    • You take no damage from close-range enemy attacks

  • Extra Perk
    • From now on, you will find an extra perk in every Holy Mountain

  • Extra Maximum HP From Hearts
    • Hearts bestow double the maximum HP.

  • Extra Enemy Knockback
    • Your spells knock enemies back more

  • Exploding Corpses
    • Enemies explode upon death

  • Electricity Immunity
    • You are immune to electricity

  • Dissolve Powders
    • Sand and other soft, powdery materials dissolve quickly in your presence.

  • Concentrated Spells
    • Your spells have lower spread but they push you back more

  • Bombs Materialized
    • Bomb-like spells can be placed in the ITEMS space in inventory and thrown without a wand

Tier 3:

  • Revenge Tentacle
    • You call a monstrous tentacle against those who harm you

  • More Blood
    • Blood blood blood

  • Greed
    • You gain 2x gold per nugget.

  • Fire Immunity
    • You take no damage from fire.

  • Faster Movement
    • Your movement speed is increased.

  • Extra HP
    • 50% extra maximum health.

  • Critical Hit +
    • Increase critical hit chance.

  • Breathless
    • Your O2 won't run out while swimming

  • Attract Physical Items
    • Physical items such as gold nuggets gravitate towards you

  • All Seeing Eye
    • You can now see everywhere

Tier 4:

  • Saving Grace
    • If you would die and have more than 1 HP, your HP is set to 1 instead.

  • Invisibility
    • You're invisible. Stains, casting spells, kicking and taking damage removes the effect temporary

  • Homing Shot
    • All your spells home towards enemies very slightly

  • Electricity
    • You're immune to electric damage but metal and liquids around you electrify constantly. Look out!

Tier 5:

  • Permanent Shield
    • You gain a small, permanent shield.

  • Perk Lottery
    • When you pick a perk, there's a 50% chance the others won't disappear.

  • Extra Life
    • Upon death you respawn with 100 health.
